Data granularity refers to the level of detail. Depending on the requirements, multiple levels of detail may be present. Many data warehouses have at least dual levels of granularity.
🔍 Daily Detail Use Cases
• Fraud detection analysis
• Individual order tracking
• Customer service inquiries
• Real-time inventory updates
• Detailed audit trails
📊 Monthly Summary Use Cases
• Customer behavior analysis
• Sales performance reports
• Inventory planning
• Marketing campaign ROI
• Operational efficiency metrics
📈 Quarterly Summary Use Cases
• Strategic business planning
• Market trend analysis
• Customer segmentation
• Financial forecasting
• Executive dashboards

🎯 Business Questions by Granularity Level

Daily Detail Questions

"What specific products did customer X buy on March 15th?"

"Which orders were shipped late yesterday?"

"What was the exact sequence of a customer's shopping session?"

Monthly Summary Questions

"What's our average order value this month vs last month?"

"Which customers are our top spenders this month?"

"How did product category sales perform this month?"

Quarterly Summary Questions

"What's our customer acquisition cost this quarter?"

"How is our market share trending over quarters?"

"What's the quarterly customer lifetime value by segment?"
