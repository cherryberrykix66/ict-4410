Current Data Fragmentation Problems

Siloed Systems
Policy data lives in one system, claims in another, and customer service in a third. No unified view of our business operations.
Inconsistent Reporting
Different departments report conflicting metrics for the same time periods, creating confusion and mistrust in our data.
Delayed Decision Making
Executive dashboards lag 2-3 weeks behind actual performance, preventing timely strategic decisions.
Wasted Analysis Time
Our analysts spend 60% of their time gathering and reconciling data instead of providing valuable insights.
Compliance Risks
Inconsistent regulatory reporting creates compliance risks and potential penalties from state insurance regulators.
Missed Opportunities
Cannot perform cross-functional analysis to identify trends, risks, and opportunities across our business.

The Solution: Unified Data Warehouse Architecture
We need to transform how Rocky Mountain Insurance Group leverages data for competitive advantage. A centralized data warehouse will eliminate silos, ensure consistent reporting, and enable real-time decision making across all departments.
